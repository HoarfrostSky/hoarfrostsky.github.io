2022 Album

To Wither

Nycrama's first album TO WITHER tells a prophecy, a vision of what's to come, or what may already have happened. An empty man leaves his empty world to embark on a quest for power. Guided by forgotten words, he attains Nycrama's Seventh Scroll -that of sorrow and decay- which grants him inmortality.

The album serves as a psychological study as he confronts his demons, his now forgotten human nature and, in the end, time itself. The world of the divine shall not be disrupted by mere mortals. Thus, consequences come.
