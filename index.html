Hoarfrost Sky

Who am I?

I am Carlos Garrido Guerrero, an artist whose works and projects span across many different fields. Hoarfrost Sky stands as the reflection of such artistic restlessness and needs.

Underneath the snow, life and death decay, drifting within the cold flames of oblivion. The only that remains is a hoarfrost sky, untarnished by time.

Game Development

Direction and development of small indie games that capture a unique and decaying essence. All games can be found on Itch.io.

Music

My music can be found across all streaming platforms. I'm also the guitarrist and main composer in Prog-Deathcore band Nycrama.

Writing & Worldbuilding

Short stories and novels that break the bounds between surrealism, horror and beauty. Creating the currently expanding dark-fantasy worldbuilding project Arakar.

Illustration & Pixel Art

Many of my works serve as the assets and building blocks of my games and projects, such as depicting the different stories and cultures that can be found in Arakar.

Video production

Creation of artistic, amateur videos that complete the message of my works, be it trailers, videoclips or short movies.

Urban Exploration

One of the main inspirations that fuel my imagination and experience. Truth is only found in decadence.
