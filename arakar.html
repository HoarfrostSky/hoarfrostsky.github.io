Home Arakar

A genre-bending currently-expanding worldbuilding project spanning millenias.

Arakar, in one form or the other, has been in my mind since I was just a little kid. This universe has matured along with me, and flourished into what it is today. Years of deep lore, mind-breaking worlds and heartful stories are all stored and developed inside this project, which serves as the foundation for many experiences that are currently being planned and worked on.
