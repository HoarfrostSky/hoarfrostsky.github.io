Atmospheric Black Metal / Experimental Blackgaze project from Spain.

Where it all began. Hoarfrost Sky started as a personal musical project in 2020, during the COVID lockdown and at a very vulnerable point in my life. Creating music serves as a catalyst of my artistic expression in many other fields, which often feeds back into the songs I create.

    2023 Album

    Of a Distant Hue

    The Split EP "The Woods of Wither" served as a great way to achieve a sound, in terms of composition and production, in which I'm very comfortable, yet still open to experimentation and trying out new things. On "Of A Distant Hue" I wanted to try a more classical approach to atmospheric black metal, but at the same time keeping things fresh. Ambient and avant-garde take a bit of a backseat to give way to dozens of melancholic riffs and melodies, mixed with a few surprises on the way.

    On a side note, I explore abandoned places on my spare time. It has changed my life on so many ways as I consider it such a beautiful origin of emotions and experiencies that one can't find any other way. Many of the themes explored in this album are based on such experiencies.

    Oneiric Birth

    Through the inmutable forces of nature, those which command the stars into the blackest eternity, those which encompass the dance of falling dust, those which guide information through the cosmos, trapped in the most somber of dreams.

    A system complex enough to understand itself, yet so futile their words. Drunk on the lie of life, the being realizes the truth past the endless road, a veil drenched in the vomit of their ignorance, infested with maggots and tears all the same. In its despair, the complex system traps itself in its dream, truth finally revealed, and rebirths anew.

    An Oneiric Birth.

    2020 Album

    Words to Welcome a Dying Age

    "Since I was a boy, I wished for the firmament to be black, and so I wasted my life stealing the stars. But I was wrong, I only needed to steal my own eyes."

    Based on a character from the novel "Tronar" by Carlos Garrido, Words to Welcome a Dying Age serves as a window to his deserted mind, broken into pure isolation from reality. The last step to achieve freedom is taken and everything falls right into place. What once was a proud man, now a mere breathing rock on the mossy ground.

    Freedom... at last.
